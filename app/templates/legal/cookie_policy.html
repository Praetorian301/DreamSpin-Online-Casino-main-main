What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

We use cookies and similar technologies for several important purposes:

✅

Essential Functionality

Keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and ensure the site works properly

🛡️

Security & Fraud Prevention

Protect your account, detect suspicious activity, and prevent abuse

📊

Analytics & Performance

Understand how you use our site and improve your experience

🎯

Personalization

Remember your settings and provide personalized content

📋 Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Purpose: Essential for the website to function. Without these, services you've asked for can't be provided.

Examples:

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Security and authentication
  • Load balancing across servers
  • Shopping cart functionality

Can you disable them? No - These are essential and cannot be disabled.

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Examples:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on pages
  • Error messages encountered
  • Device and browser information

Can you disable them? Yes - You can opt out via cookie settings.

Duration: Up to 2 years

Third parties: Google Analytics (anonymized)

3. Functionality Cookies

Purpose: Remember your preferences and choices to provide enhanced features.

Examples:

  • Language preference
  • Dark/light mode selection
  • Username for login
  • Game settings and favorites
  • Volume and sound preferences

Can you disable them? Yes - But this may affect some features.

Duration: Up to 1 year

4. Targeting & Advertising Cookies

Purpose: Track your browsing to show relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

Examples:

  • Displaying personalized promotions
  • Limiting ad frequency
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Retargeting campaigns

Can you disable them? Yes - You can opt out via cookie settings.

Duration: Up to 2 years

Note: Currently, DreamSpin does not use advertising cookies.

⚙️ How to Control Cookies

Option 1: Use Our Cookie Settings

You can manage your cookie preferences using our cookie consent tool:

Option 2: Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how:

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and site data

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent the website from functioning properly.

📱 Mobile Devices

For mobile browsers and apps:

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
  • Mobile Apps: Check your device's app settings for cookie/tracking controls

🔗 Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

Web Beacons / Pixels

Session Replay

Records user interactions to help us improve the user experience and fix bugs.

Fingerprinting

Uses device characteristics for security and fraud prevention (not for tracking).

⚠️ Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals.

We respect your privacy choices, but we don't currently respond to DNT signals. Instead, please use our cookie settings or browser controls to manage your preferences.
