Know Your Customer - Identity Verification Process

What is KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process required by law to prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities. It involves confirming your identity and ensuring you meet our eligibility requirements.

We take your privacy seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information.

📄 Required Documents

To complete KYC verification, you'll need to provide the following:

1️⃣ Proof of Identity

One of the following:

  • Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, national ID card)
  • Must be valid (not expired)
  • Must show your full name, date of birth, and photo
  • All four corners must be visible
  • Text must be clear and readable

2️⃣ Proof of Address

One of the following (issued within last 3 months):

  • Utility bill (electricity, gas, water)
  • Bank statement
  • Government-issued document with address
  • Lease agreement
  • Must show your full name and current address

3️⃣ Proof of Payment (Optional)

May be required for certain payment methods:

  • Photo of credit/debit card (front only, middle 6 digits covered)
  • Screenshot of e-wallet account
  • Cryptocurrency wallet ownership proof

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When do I need to complete KYC?

KYC is required before your first withdrawal or when requested by our compliance team. You can play and deposit without KYC, but you must complete it to withdraw funds.

What if my documents are rejected?

We'll email you explaining why and what's needed. Common reasons include blurry images, expired documents, or missing information. Simply resubmit with corrected documents.

Can I play while verification is pending?

Yes! You can continue playing and depositing. Only withdrawals are restricted until verification is complete.

Do I need to verify again?

Once verified, you typically won't need to verify again unless your personal information changes or if requested for security reasons.

What if I don't have a utility bill?

Bank statements, government documents, or lease agreements work too. Contact support if you're unsure what documents to provide.

Is my information shared with third parties?

No. We never sell or share your personal information. It's only used for verification and legal compliance. See our Privacy Policy for details.
